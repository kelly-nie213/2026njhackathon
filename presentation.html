aegis Core Features
Four Lenses

Everything an attacker sees — in one scan

I
Exposure & Breaches
Crawls your public pages for staff emails, names and phone numbers, then checks each against known breach databases — and rolls them up by breach.
II
Email Spoofing
A live DNS check of SPF, DMARC, DKIM and MX: can a stranger send email that looks like it came from your real address?
III
Web & Code Security
TLS certificate and HTTP security headers, graded A–F, plus a static scan of the JavaScript your site ships — leaked keys, XSS, outdated libraries.
IV
Threat Reputation
Live blocklists (OpenPhish, URLhaus, Safe Browsing, VirusTotal) check whether your domain is flagged for malware or phishing right now.
aegis Under the Hood
Real Sources, Real Checks

Where the findings come from

What we gather
  • Breadth-first crawl of your public pages
  • Staff emails, names and phone numbers
  • Per-email breach lookup via XposedOrNot
  • A domain-level rollup of breaches found
  • Live DNS records: SPF, DMARC, DKIM, MX
What we assess
  • TLS certificate validity and expiry
  • CSP, HSTS, X-Frame-Options and more
  • Shipped JavaScript: secrets, XSS, old libraries
  • Malware & phishing blocklist reputation
  • A single, prioritized action plan
